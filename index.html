Hi! I'm Lars.

I design products, services and help companies become more human centered. I do design research, strategy, UX, visual design and code. I start from people's needs and goals but also consider economical viability and technical feasibility.

I've come up with ideas for new games with Playstation, developed digital strategy for kitchens with IKEA, prototyped a new food delivery service, piloted a hotel experience with Crown Plaza, built tools for people in poverty with the CFPB and shipped apps as an independent developer/designer.

I care about a sustainable future, electric vehicles and renewable energy.⚡️
