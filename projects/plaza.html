Crowne Plaza

Ignition is and new meeting and working experience, housed in the lobby of Crowne Plaza hotels that incorporates a new suite of brand, space, furniture, service role, food and beverage offers, digital tools, and business strategy.

The concept is designed to increase travelers productivity and launched with two pilots in Atlanta.
