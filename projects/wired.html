Wired

We designed Wired's site as part of it's launch in Germany. Wired is known for its high quality content and visuals and we wanted to celebrate that in the design. To do this we focused on creating a great reading experience. Beautiful typography, big, easy to read type and huge imagery.

Content is organized in collections of topics like tech, design and business as well as types of content like columns, featured and latest. During events like CES temporary collections can be added. The site is fully responsive and articles are swipeable to easily go to the next or previous.
